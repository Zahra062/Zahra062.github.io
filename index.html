Autumnn Coffee Recipes

Read Time: 10 minutes

Its that time of year where the leaves turn into shades of gold, orange, and brown and the temperature starts to drop. It is the season to be cozy and curl up with a hot drink to keep you warm this Autumn. Without further ado, lets get into our favourite autumn coffee recipes apart from a pumpkin spiced latte!.

Maple Spiced Latte

Ingredients:

  • 1 ounce maple syrup
  • 6 ounces of milk
  • Pinch of cinammon
  • Pinch of nutmeg
  • Pinch of clove
  • 2 ounces strong coffee (espresso)
    • Can use a coffee maker to make espresso

Instructions: Mix maple syrup, coffee, and spices in a mug. Stir very well, break up dry spice clumps. Add frothed milk to the mixture. Top with a pinch of your favorite spice.

Oden, 2019

Nescafe Chocolate and Orange Cappucino

Ingredients:

  • 4 - 6 tsp NESCAFÉ Original Decaff (dissolved in 6tbsp boiling water)
  • 1 tbsp chocolate fudge syrup
  • 2 tbsp sugar free orange syrup
  • 400 ml frothy hot milk
  • Cocoa powder or grated chocolate, for dusting
  • 2 Quality Street Orange Matchmakers, to stir

Equipment:

  • Jug
  • 2 Mugs

Instructions: Mix the NESCAFÉ Original Decaff and syrups in a small jug. Pour into 2 large cappuccino cups or mugs. Top with foamed milk and dust with cocoa powder. Serve with a Quality Street Orange Matchmaker to stir and melt into the coffee.

Nescafe, 2023

Caramel Apple Latte

Ingredients:

Caramel apple sauce:

  • ¼ cup apple butter
  • ¼ cup caramel sauce

    • For the latte:

    • 2 oz caramel apple sauce (or to taste)
    • 2 oz brewed espresso (or strong coffee)
    • 4 oz steamed milk of choice
    • Whipped cream
    • Additional caramel, for serving
    • Ground cinnamon, for dusting

    Instructions: Spoon the caramel apple sauce into the bottom of a serving glass or mug. Top off with the hot espresso and stir well to melt the caramel apple mixture in the hot espresso. Top off with the steamed milk, whipped cream, a drizzle of caramel, and a light dusting of cinnamon. Enjoy immediately.

    Molly, 2021
